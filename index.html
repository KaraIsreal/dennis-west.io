What We've Accomplished

WORKING WITH OUR CITIES

We are glad to report to you that DWM and our volunteers have seized several opportunities to work with the City of Rancho Cucamonga with what we call “Operation Clean Up” which helps clean up our surrounding communities, and to assist those who were widowed, single parents, and others in desperate need of man power that we were able to provide.

FOOD & CLOTHES GIVEAWAYS / TOYS & FREE HAIR CUTS

We have partnered with World Vision in delivering food bags with hearty meals inside to the door steps of many people in need. Plus, through our events like The Inland Empire Family Summit we have provided free haircuts, toys, school supplies, “Blessing Bags” filled with soaps, shampoos, and other hygiene products.

TEACHING & RELEASING THE ANOINTING

DWM holds Men of Faith, and Women of Faith prayer meetings that have lifted the hearts and minds of those needing encouragement.

VACATION BIBLE SCHOOL / CHILDREN’S PROGRAMS

The partners of DWM stepped in financially and made it possible for several children that could not otherwise afford to attend Bible School to attend our VBS, and the lives of many of our youth were changed forever, through bible teaching, field trips, and more! In a combined effort with the Good Steward Christian Preschool, we have put our hands to the plow as it relates to pouring into our children the kind of values our society needs.

TRAINING & EDUCATION

During a trip to the Living Word Christian Center, I was able to sit for 2 complete days at the feet of our great teacher and leader Dr. Bill Winston to pray, learn and bring down Kingdom strategies on how we can vastly win more souls and make more disciples in these last days! This training will surely help us use effective strategies to win the lost and identify resources to meet more needs.

BAPTISING IN JESUS NAME

DWM and Gospel Vision Christian Center Church collectively baptized in Jesus Name not only the member of our church, but we have baptized others who have been saved by our evangelistic efforts.
